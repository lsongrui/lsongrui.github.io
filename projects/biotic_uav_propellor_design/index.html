Abstract#

In this study, the drag reduction propeller with serrated leading edge is designed based on the bionics principle-the fin of humpback whale. It is divided into 4 steps, CFD simulation of three kinds of straight airfoil, propeller design, propeller strength design, propeller modeling, propeller 3D printing, from the design and selection of the foundation straight airfoil to the design and research of the three-dimensional propeller. In the last step, a simple experimental verification was carried out. Finally, a conclusion is drawn that the standard-sin leading edge airfoil shows a better characteristic line and the bionics propeller possesses a higher proficiency.

Key pictures and conclusions#

Airfoil simulation#

CFD simulation of three kinds of straight airfoil are performed:

  • before the angle of attack, the drag of the two concave leading edge models is significantly reduced compared with the standard leading edge
  • the sinus model possesses the best lift drag profile, and this will be taken to 3D model

3D Propeller design#

modeling#

  • Leading edge: $-45 z=0.5/12\times9.3133\times sin(0.3373\times3\times y)$

  • Torsion center: wing center

  • Torsion angle: $-45$-$0$ degrees linear distribution

this is the final propeller design with optimised propelling efficiency before the critical rotational speed.

by further strength analysis, the critical rotational speed is $n_{max} = 7339.27 r/min$
